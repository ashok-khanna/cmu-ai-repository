JACAL: Symbolic math system written in Scheme.

lang/scheme/code/math/jacal/
JACAL is a symbolic mathematics system for the simplification and manipulation of equations and single and multiple valued algebraic expressions constructed of numbers, variables, radicals, and algebraic functions, differential, and holonomic functions. In addition, vectors and matrices of the above objects are included. JACAL is written in Scheme. If you find JACAL useful, please support the development and maintenance of JACAL by sending money to Aubrey.
