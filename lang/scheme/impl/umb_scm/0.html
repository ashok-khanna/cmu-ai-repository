UMB Scheme: Portable Scheme from UMass/Boston.

lang/scheme/impl/umb_scm/
UMB Scheme is a portable implementation of Scheme implemented in C. It conforms to the R4RS and IEEE P1178 standards. It includes a simple editor and debugger. It gives reasonable run-time performance, is quite portable, and is suitable for use in undergraduate language courses. A port of UMB Scheme 2.05 to the Amiga is also included.
