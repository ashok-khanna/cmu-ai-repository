SMG SCM: Adds VMS SMG screen management routines to SCM.

lang/scheme/impl/scm/smg_scm/
SMG-SCM is a package that adds VMS SMG screen management routines to SCM. (SMG is VMS's equivalent of curses.) Source code, documentation, and examples are included.
