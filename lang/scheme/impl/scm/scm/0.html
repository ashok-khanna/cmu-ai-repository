SCM: Portable implementation of a R4RS Scheme in C.

lang/scheme/impl/scm/scm/
SCM is a portable implementation of Scheme written in C. SCM conforms to the Revised^4 Report on the Algorithmic Language Scheme and the IEEE P1178 specification. SCM supports ASCII and EBCDIC. For those without ftp access, an IBM PC floppy disk with the source files and MSDOS and i386 executables is available for $99 from Aubrey Jaffer (see address below). If you find SCM useful, please support the development and maintenance of SCM by sending money to Aubrey, even if you got SCM by anonymous ftp.
