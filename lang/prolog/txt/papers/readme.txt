Papers in this collection include:

   Alan Borning, Bjorn N. Freeman-Benson, and Molly Wilson, "Constraint
   hierarchies", Lisp and Symbolic Computation 5:223-270, 1992.

   Philippe Codognet and Vijay Saraswat, "Abduction in Concurrent
   Constraint Programming", March 1992.

   Vijay A. Saraswat, Johan de Kleer, and Brian C. Williams, "ATMS-based
   constraint programming", Xerox PARC, October 24, 1991.

   Joxan Jaffar, Spiro Michaylov, Peter J. Stuckey, and Roland H.C. Yap,
   "An Abstract Machine for CLP(R)", November 1991.

   Thierry Le Provost and Mark Wallace, "Generalised Constraint
   Propagation Over the CLP Scheme" ECRC Technical Report ECRC-92-1,
   February 1993. Also appears in the Journal of Logic Programming, 1993.

   Vijay A. Saraswat, Martin Rinard, and Prakash Panangaden, "Semantic
   foundations of concurrent constraint programming", October 1990. Also
   appears in POPL-91.

   Bjorn N. Freeman-Benson and Alan Borning, "Integrating Constraints
   with an Object-Oriented Language", Proceedings of the 1992 European
   Conference on Object-Oriented Programming, pages 268-286, 1992.

   Vijay A. Saraswat, "Concurrency in the Lambda-calculus: Higher-order
   Concurrent Constraint Programming", Xerox PARC, December 4, 1991.

   Prakash Panageden, Vijay Saraswat, P.J. Scott, and R.A.G. Seely,
   "What is a Constraint System?", December 8, 1991.

Some of these papers come from the "Constraint Programming Paper
Archive" located at Aarhus University, Denmark, and accessible by
anonymous ftp as ftp.daimi.aau.dk:pub/CLP/. For further information on
the archive, contact Brian H. Mayoh <brian@daimi.aau.dk>.
