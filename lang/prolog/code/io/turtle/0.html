Prolog turtle graphics.

lang/prolog/code/io/turtle/
This is a simple turtle graphics program for vt100 terminals written by David Lau-Kee and originally posted to the Prolog Digest. A few syntactic errors were corrected by Salleh Mustaffa, University of Manchester. There may be some problems due to control characters in the program not being properly transmitted through email in the original posting. A limited subset of turtle graphics capabilities is implemented: You can tell the turtle to turn 90 degrees clockwise or anticlockwise, and to move forward X steps. Backtracking over these primitives causes them to be undone.
