This directory contains a BibTeX bibliography for the journal "Information and Computation" published by Academic Press and edited by Albert R. Meyer. It covers the years 1982 through the present (volumes 52 through the present). For information on ordering back issues, write to Academic Press, Inc. Attn: Jacquie Mahon Journal Division/Back Volumes 1250 Sixth Avenue San Diego, CA 92101
