lang/others/dylan/news/
This directory contains archives for the comp.lang.dylan newsgroup. The new articles are archived once a week, in a gzipped tar file of the form YY_MM_DD.tgz, where YY, MM, and DD are replaced with the year, month, and day, respectively. Most archiving began with 94_09_14.tgz, which includes articles since 23-JUL-94. The archives from September 1992 to September 1994 (92_p1.gz through 94_p3.gz) were obtained from the cambridge.apple.com archive site.
