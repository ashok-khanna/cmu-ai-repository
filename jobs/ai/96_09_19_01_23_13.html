They are looking to capture, represent, manage, and deploy their building systems knowledge to enhance their service and product offerings and create new offerings to better serve their customers. They are also looking for hands-on knowledge engineers to join their knowledge program. Johnson Controls, Inc. Controls Group, headquartered in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, is a global market leader in non-residential building control systems. Their industry-leading Metasys facility management system integrates lighting, fire, and security systems and takes advantage of the latest developments in intelligent building technology. The Controls Group is also the largest provider of integrated facility management services in the world. Johnson Controls employees are on site, working full time at hundreds of customer buildings around the world, to simultaneously improve facility performance and indoor environments while reducing operating costs. The job candidates should have B.S., M.S. or Ph.D. degrees in engineering or computer science, with a concentration in knowledge based systems. The candidate should also have experience in successfully building and deploying knowledge based systems in industry. The knowledge based systems R&D effort will work closely with, and integrate with ongoing development of knowledge based systems and infrastructure to support developing these systems, as well as R&D efforts in advanced controls systems, computer based models of building systems, and quality building environments. The Controls Group is located just a few blocks from the Milwaukee waterfront on the west coast of Lake Michigan. This is one of the most publicly accessible waterfronts of any major city. Miles of public beaches, marinas, community boating, and summer festival grounds. There are a wide range of fine and performing arts venues, major sports franchises, three major universities, and access to the great outdoors of Wisconsin.
