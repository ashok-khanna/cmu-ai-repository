The research and development work of the Operations Scheduling project is aimed at a problem of vital importance for the US space program: dramatically reducing space mission operating costs through the use of automation and Artificial Intelligence software technology. Currently we are working on various project including the Deep Space 1 (DS1) mission. Scheduled for launch in 1998 DS1 will be the first mission Remote Agent, a highly autonomous flight operations software. Remote Agent will generate plans on-board on the basis of a few abstract goals, will execute generated plans, will monitor for execution failures and will autonomously recover from them. The Operations Scheduling project is working on on-board Planner/Scheduler component of the Remote Agent, which will be responsible for the on-board expansion of the detailed execution plans on the basis of a few, abstract goals communicated to the spacecraft from ground. The successful candidate will be joining a highly motivated team in a very dynamic environment. The successful candidate must satisfy the following requirements: - American citizenship or permanent residency (this is a necessary condition for employment!); - MS/PhD (or experiential equivalent) in Computer Science or related fields; - proficiency with the development of object-oriented systems; - proficiency in the use of C++ and Unix; - familiarity with search techniques, constraint propagation, operations research, planning and scheduling; - good written and verbal skills. The following skills will be consider as definite advantages for the successful candidate: - good working knowledge of Lisp; - good working knowledge of VxWorks; Motivation to learn quickly about the previous topics is a very strong requirement. The NASA Ames Research Center is located near Mountain View, in northern California. The position offers a competitive compensation package, excellent benefits, the opportunity to join a well-motivated, hard-working team, and the chance to directly impact the future of the US space program.
