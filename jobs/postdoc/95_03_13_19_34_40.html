Witten) Subject: Lectureship/Postdoc in Machine Learning Date: 12 Mar 1995 21:22:39 GMT Organization: The University of Waikato THE UNIVERSITY OF WAIKATO Hamilton POST DOCTORAL FELLOWSHIP/PART-TIME LECTURESHIP IN COMPUTER SCIENCE Applications are invited for a post-doctoral fellowship/part-time lectureship in Computer Science at the University of Waikato. Applicants should hold a recent PhD in Computer Science or equivalent qualification. They should have a research background in some area of machine learning. The position is half-time PDF and half-time lectureship. The successful applicant will be required to teach a half teaching load in the Computer Science department. He or she will also be expected to conduct research in the area of machine learning and to participate in ongoing projects in this area. Projects include the application of machine learning to various problems in agriculture, the development of new machine learning algorithms, programming by demonstration, and theoretical foundations of learning based on the MDL principle. The current salary range is $37,440 - $49,088 per annum. The position is for a one year period. Enquiries of an academic nature may be made to the machine learning group co-ordinator, Professor Ian Witten, telephone 64-7-838-4246, fax 64-7-838-4155, e-mail address: ihw@waikato.ac.nz. Information on the method of application and conditions of appointment can be obtained from Personnel and Management Services, The University of Waikato, Private Bag 3105, Hamilton, New Zealand, telephone 64-7-838-4003, fax 64-7 856-0135, e-mail personnel@waikato.ac.nz. Applications quoting reference number A95/18 should reach Personnel and Management Services by 31 March 1995. Places for appointee's children may be available in the creche run by the Campus Creche Society (Inc).
