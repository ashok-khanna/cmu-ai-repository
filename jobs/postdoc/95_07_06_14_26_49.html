The successful applicant will be required to teach in both lower level and upper level computing science courses (including service courses) and at least lower level Mathematics courses. The Department of Mathematical Sciences is undergraduate and includes Mathematics, Computing, and Physics. Teaching experience is very much preferred and strong academic and personal references are required. Preference will be given to a person with expertise in one or more of: operating systems, software engineering, AI, and logic programming, but versatility is more important than the specific speciality. Minimum academic qualification: Ph.D. (or equivalent) in Computing Science, Informatics, or MACS. Trinity Western University is a Christian liberal arts university, affiliated with the Evangelical Free Church of Canada. It is a full member of the A.U.C.C. Candidates must be able to support the University's Statement of Faith, and model its Community Standards agreement.
