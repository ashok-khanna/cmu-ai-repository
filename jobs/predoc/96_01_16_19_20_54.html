AT&T Microelectronics in Orlando is a semiconductor manufacturing plant that owns over 350 million dollars in capital investment and employs over 1000 people. This two years program offers an excellent opportunity to earn a masters degree in Industrial Engineering while gaining valuable experience at one of the leading manufacturing companies. The Industrial Fellows receive a stipend, full tuition and fee waivers, and book allowances. In addition, Fellows will be employed as research assistants on site at AT&T Microelectronics in Orlando. The work commitment is 20 hours per week during the academic year and full time during the summer. The Fellows will work on a research project which will lead to a thesis. Applicants must have earned a BS in Industrial Engineering or related field.
