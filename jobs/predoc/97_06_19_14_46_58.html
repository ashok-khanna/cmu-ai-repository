The aim of the project is: To research and develop intelligent forecasting techniques; particularly with reference to low volume sales products To develop novel algorithms which will utilise expert system and Bayesian approaches to solve low volume forecasting problems To experiment with these techniques within TGE's software product To develop a new, more intelligent, version of the software The CASE student will undertake research work in the areas of intelligent forecasting, based on previous work undertaken at the University of Sunderland and elsewhere. The aim will be to develop a system which will use expert knowledge and beliefs as well as sales data to produce a more reliable forecast. This will involve extensive collaboration between the University and the company, TGE. The ultimate aim is to design and build new sophisticated self-learning and forecasting functionality into the system. Candidates must have at least an upper second class honours degree in Computing and/or Mathematics or Statistics or equivalent.
