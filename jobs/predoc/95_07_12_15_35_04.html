The project involves: * interfacing an off-the-shelf manipulator arm to a mobile robot * interfacing the electronics needed for control of the manipulator on the mobile base * programming the system using a real time operating system like Vx-Works * linking the mobile base and the manipulator to an off-board host computer, running a real time os, using wireless ethernet. * interfacing of external sensors like vision systems to the mobile base * developing algorithms for autonomous navigation, manipulation and retrieval of objects. Requirements: The candidate must have either a mechanical or electrical engineering background. The candidate must have had prior experience in dealing with interface and control of electrical and mechanical systems. The project requires a fairly in-depth understanding of both electronics and mechanical systems design and is a challenging prospect for students wishing to develop skills in both the fields. There are additional requirements for admission into the program. Contact Information: Dr. Sunil K.
