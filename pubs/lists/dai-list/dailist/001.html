I would appreciate it very much if you would pass it on to those who might be interested. GTE Laboratories is exploring the application of Distributed Artificial Intelligence techniques to the operation and management of telecommunication networks. Our current project involves cooperation among agents that differ in their domain knowledge. These agents interact in order to integrate the management of public telephone networks with the control by customers of private networks, as a proof-of-concept for DAI technology. This research anticipates a long term impact on this and similar application domains. We are looking for an experienced DAI researcher with the ability to contribute to both the conceptual design and the practical implementation of cooperative multiagent systems. Research in this project may include practically any aspect of multiagent behavior: cooperative planning, opportunistic reasoning, interagent communication languages, etc. The successful candidate will join a small team that is currently developing a DAI research testbed. For more information about this position, contact Robert Weihmayer at weihmayer@gte.com (on CSNET), or if more convenient, by phone at 617-466-2811. If you wish to apply, please send your resume (mentioning DAI in your cover letter) to Vanessa Stern, Employment Representative, GTE Laboratories Inc., 40 Sylvan Road, Waltham, MA 02254. An equal opportunity employer, M/F/H/V. GTE Laboratories provides a well-supported research environment, an attractive benefits package, a friendly atmosphere, proximity to an abundance of cultural centers, as well as vast opportunities for personal and professional growth.
