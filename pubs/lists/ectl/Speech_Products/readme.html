While commercial advertisements are not considered suitable as ECTL mailing material, ECTL will now accept brief (no more than 4k) adverts to be held in the anonymous ftp archive. Please submit any advertisements to: ectl-sub@snowhite.cis.uoguelph.ca (with commercial advert as the subject of the message.) The following type of information should be included: Which platform(s) the product is compatible with What exactly the product is capable of Price (please include currency) How to order (addresses, phone numbers, fax numbers, e-mail addresses, etc.) How to obtain further information.
