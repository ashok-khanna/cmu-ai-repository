The JOURNAL OF INTELLIGENT INFORMATION SYSTEMS: INTEGRATING AI AND DATABASE TECHNOLOGIES provides a forum for the publication of research papers pertaining to the integration of artificial intelligence and database management technologies, building the next generation of systems - intelligent information systems. The journal focuses on methodologies for the creation of information systems that exhibit intelligent behavior and embody intelligence in their reasoning and management processes. The JOURNAL OF INTELLIGENT INFORMATION SYSTEMS: INTEGRATING AI AND DATABASE TECHNOLOGIES is an archival, peer-reviewed, technical journal which welcomes high-quality papers on the fields of intelligent information systems. The journal publishes invited papers, workshop and conference reports, survey and tutorial articles, book reviews, and meeting announcements.
