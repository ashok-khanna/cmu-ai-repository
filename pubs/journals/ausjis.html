The Australian Journal of Information Systems (ISSN 1039-7841) is a refereed journal that publishes articles (twice a year) contributing to information systems theory and practice. Manuscripts from all areas related to information systems are being sought to reflect the needs and interests of the information systems community. Manuscripts are acknowledged on receipt. AJIS is published twice a year by the Department of Business Systems, University of Wollongong in conjunction with the School of Computing and Information Technology, Monash University and AJET Publishing.
