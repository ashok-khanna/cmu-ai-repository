This directory and its subdirectories contains information relating to publications from Kluwer Academic Publishers. This includes LaTeX style files for use when producing books and submitting articles to journals. Adherance to these files will ensure fast publication of your work. Other directories contain the table of contents of the computer science and electrical engineering journals and books published by Kluwer.
