Mamdani, E.H. and Gaines, B.R., Fuzzy Reasoning and its Applications, New York: Academic

Negoita, Constantin Virgil, Fuzzy Systems, Cybernetics and Systems Series, Abacus Press, 1981.

Sugeno, Michio, Industrial Applications of Fuzzy Control, New York: North-Holland, 1985. 270

Zimmermann, Hans J., Fuzzy Set Theory and its Applications, Boston MA: Kluwer-Nijhoff
