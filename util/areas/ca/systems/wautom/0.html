Wautom: Cellular automata simulator for PCs.

areas/ca/systems/wautom/
WAUTOM is a simulator for 1-dimensional elementary binary cellular automata (3-cell neighbors), with Wolfram's rules 0 to 255. WA2D is similar, but for 2d automata (4-cell neighbors). WAUTNN simulates the behavior of 1d binary CA with 5-cell neighbors. 2DHODGE is a simulator for the 2D "hodge-podge machine" with generic k states and other parameters.
