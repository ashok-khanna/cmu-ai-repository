  Communication Association Conference, May 1988)                               
                                                                                
                                                                                
     1. Krippendorff, K. Cybernetics.  Entry in International                   
  Encyclopedia of Communication.  New York: Oxford University Press,

  for Cybernetics.  American Society for Cybernetics, 1983.
