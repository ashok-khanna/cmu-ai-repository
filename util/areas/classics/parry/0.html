PARRY: Paranoia mental hospital patient.

areas/classics/parry/
This is the original implementation of Parry, in MLISP (meta-lisp) for a PDP-10. It will not work in Common Lisp and would require a significant amount of porting (parts are written in PDP-10 assembly code). [If anybody ports this to Common Lisp, please send a copy to the CMU AI Repository.] Also included in this directory is a sample of some amusing dialogue between PARRY and ELIZA.
