Implementation of Conway's Game of Life for Sun.

areas/alife/systems/life/hatch/
This directory contains a fast pixel-level implementation of Conway's Game of Life a on a torus for a black and white Sun. Runs at 6.8 frames per second for the full screen on a Sparc.
