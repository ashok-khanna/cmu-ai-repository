Origin:   

   clr.nmsu.edu:/pub/misc/companies.Z
This directory contains a list of over 50,000 corporations and their country of origin. The format is: COMPANY | COUNTRY All of the companies with UNITED STATES as their origin came off of the Compact Disclosure disk "Corporate Information on Public Companies Filing with the SEC." All other names came off the National Trade Data Bank, distributed by the U.S. Department of Commerce. It appears that the string "XXXX S" should be interpreted as "XXXX'S" However, this is not always the case.
CD-ROM: Author(s): Eric Iverson Keywords: Companies, Corpora, Natural Language Processing
