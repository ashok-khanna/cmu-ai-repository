This directory contains archives of many of the NLP-related newsgroups. The new articles in each newsgroup are archived once a week, in a gzipped tar file of the form YY_MM_DD.tgz, where YY, MM, and DD are replaced with the year, month, and day, respectively. Most newsgroups begin with 94_09_14.tgz, which includes articles since 23-JUL-94, but some may include articles before that.
