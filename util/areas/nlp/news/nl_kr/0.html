This directory contains archives for the comp.ai.nlang-know-rep newsgroup. The new articles are archived once a week, in a gzipped tar file of the form YY_MM_DD.tgz, where YY, MM, and DD are replaced with the year, month, and day, respectively. Most archiving began with 94_10_07.tgz, which includes articles since 23-JUL-94.
