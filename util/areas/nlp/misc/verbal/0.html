Verbalist: Demonstration of inflectional forms of English verbs

areas/nlp/misc/verbal/
VERBALIST is a "verb-building robot" that demonstrates various inflectional forms of English verbs, and indicates as well some of their collocational restrictions. Includes an interesting summary of the overt rules used.
