COGNATE: Identifies related words in bilingual texts.

areas/nlp/mt/cognate/
COGNATE is the implementation of a prototype algorithm for identifying related words across languages. Given the same list of words in two different languages, COGNATE will determine which words are likely to be regularly derivable from each other, and which are not.
