CSLI: CSLI's Natural Language Processing Bibliography

areas/nlp/doc/bib/csli/
This directory contains CSLI's 3,300 entry linguistics bibliography database in bib/tib/refer format. It is heavily slanted towards phonetics/phonology but with a fair amount of morphology, syntax, and semantics thrown in, especially if your interests are computational. Contributions are welcome, but should be in tib format.
