areas/robotics/news/
This directory contains archives for the comp.robotics newsgroup. The new articles are archived once a week, in a gzipped tar file of the form YY_MM_DD.tgz, where YY, MM, and DD are replaced with the year, month, and day, respectively. Most archiving began with 94_09_14.tgz, which includes articles since 23-JUL-94. The archives from May 1990 through August 1994 were obtained by reorganizing the contents of the wilma.cs.brown.edu archive.
