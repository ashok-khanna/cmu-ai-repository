ART-1: Simulation of ART-1 Neural Networks

areas/neural/systems/art/art1/
This directory contains a simulation of ART-1 Neural Networks. Adaptive Resonance Theory (ART) networks, as proposed by Carpenter and Grossberg, form clusters and are trained without supervision.
