GAMusic: Genetic Algorithm to Evolve Musical Melodies

areas/genetic/ga/systems/gamusic/
GAMusic is a user-friendly interactive demonstration of a simple genetic algorithm (GA). Here, the GA generates short melodies and the user assigns the fitnesses. The iterative stepping, mutation frequency and recombination frequency are all controlled by the user. Each series of musical notes is represented in binary form in an array 128 elements in length. This allows a maximum of 30 notes per melody and provides a solution space with approximately 3.4 * 10^38 possible melodies. [Intended as an introduction to GAs; may not be of interest to experienced GA programmers.]
