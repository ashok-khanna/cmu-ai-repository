SES: Simple Evolution Strategy

areas/genetic/ga/systems/ses/
SES (Simple Evolution Strategy) is a parameter optimization program based on Evolution Strategies (ES). It is a more or less traditional ES implementation, except for: - correlated mutations not implemented - global recombination not implemented - geometric intermediate recombination added for variances sigma[i] - discrete recombination on both parameters and variances is performed simultaneously, i.e. sigma[i] and x[i] are taken from one parent at each position i.
