wxCLIPS: Simple graphical front end to CLIPS

areas/expert/systems/clips/wxclips/
wxCLIPS provides a simple graphical front end to CLIPS 5.1 and 6.0, and in addition contains a suite of extra functions for creating GUI applications in CLIPS.
