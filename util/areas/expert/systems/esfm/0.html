ESFM: Prolog Expert System for Forestry Management

areas/expert/systems/esfm/
This directory contains a small expert system for forestry management, written in Prolog. The rules are stored as Prolog clauses, with inference occurring by Prolog executing the rules. Inference is depth-first, backward-chaining, with no explanations of why ESFM asked a question nor how it came to a conclusion.
