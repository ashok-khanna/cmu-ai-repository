Fview: Speech trajectory analysis and display

areas/speech/systems/fview/
Fview is a GUI program for speech trajectory analysis and display. The program is used for projecting higher dimensional data onto the screen of the computer and manipulating it in various ways. It is particularly useful (and designed for) speech data in the form of trajectories in twelve dimensional space.
