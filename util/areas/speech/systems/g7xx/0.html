G711_G721_G723: CCITT ulaw and ADPCM compression coders.

areas/speech/systems/g7xx/
This directory contains the reference implementations of the ulaw and ADPCM coders as described by the CCITT (International Telegraph and Telephone Consultative Committee) G.711, G.721 and G.723 voice compression standards. The implementations were written by Sun Microsystems and released to the public domain.
