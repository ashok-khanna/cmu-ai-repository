TILEWORLD: Planning testbed and simulator

areas/testbeds/tilewrld/
TILEWORLD is a planning testbed/simulator developed at SRI International by Martha Pollack, Michael Frank and Marc Ringuette. TILEWORLD originally ran under Lucid CL, but was later extended and ported to Allegro CL by Badr H. Al-Badr and Steve Hanks. It includes an X interface.
