For your newsgroups file: comp.org.lisp-users Association of Lisp Users related discussions. The charter, culled from the call for votes: Discussion of general topics relevant to the Association of Lisp Users (ALU) (including Lisp Vendors), in particular centered on membership issues, future conferences, publications, and Lisp advocacy issues. Overlap with comp.lang.lisp.*, and other groups this message is cross- posted to should be minimal, as this is NOT intended to be a technical, but an organizational group. The ALU mailing list will be bidirectionally gatewayed into this newsgroup. Participation will not be limited to current ALU members.
