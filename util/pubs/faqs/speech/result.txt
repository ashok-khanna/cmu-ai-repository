Voting on comp.speech is over.  

comp.speech (unmoderated)
   This newsgroup will be for the discussion of issues related to speech 
   technology - relevant research, application issues and related matters.


RESULTS

The vote ran for 3 weeks ending 22nd Sept 1992.  This article contains a full
list of Yes and No votes received.  If you voted and your name does not appear
correctly in the list then please send a message indicating the circumstances
to either andrewh@ee.su.oz.au OR speech_v@ee.su.oz.au.

Total votes cast:       481

Total YES votes:        474
Total NO votes:           7

The vote passes with a 98.5% Yes vote and a 467 vote margin.

Under the USENET guidelines there is now a 5 day waiting period in which
problems with the vote can be raised.  If there are no major objections within
this waiting period the newsgroup creation message will be issued.
