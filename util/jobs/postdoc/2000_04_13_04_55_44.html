COMPUTER SCIENTIST / LISP PROGRAMMER Vacancy 21.2.00 A challenging position is available for a Computer Scientist/programmer with experience in Common Lisp and CLOS. Knowledge of the Macintosh OS, MCL, MIDI and audio applications is an advantage. He/she will work on the development of models, tools and demonstration programs for computational modeling of music cognition and will need an open and helpful attitude to the other members of the team. In our environment, next to Lisp-only applications, MCL is used to control other applications via AppleScript. We are not looking for a hacker, but for someone who can maintain an up-to-date and critical view of new developments in the Lisp community. The design of WWW interfaces (Java) may also become part of the job. Appointment will be full-time or 8/10 for two years. Depending on experience the salary will be between Dfl. 4304,-- and Dfl. 5931,-- gross per month, on full-time basis. EXPERIMENTAL PSYCHOLOGIST Vacancy 21.3.00 For empirical work on categorical rhythm perception (identification and discrimination tasks) we are looking for someone with experience in designing and running experiments. Data analysis will be one of the tasks using (Macintosh) statistical packages. Experience in conducting automated experiments is requested. Affinity with music cognition work is an advantage. We offer a position in an exciting interdisciplinary team. Appointment will be full-time or 8/10 for two years. Depending on experience the salary will be between Dfl. 4110,-- and Dfl. 6516,-- gross per month, on full-time basis.
