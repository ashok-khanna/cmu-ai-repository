Thank you, Mark Johnson Postdoctoral Research Associates Chemical Informatics and Data Mining We are seeking candidates for two Postdoctoral Research Associate positions in the Computer-Aided Drug Discovery Unit at Pharmacia & Upjohn, Kalamazoo, Michigan, to carry out research in chemical informatics and data mining. The research in chemical informatics will involve development of new structure representations for use in molecular similarity and diversity studies and for browsing large compound databases. Work in data mining will be directed towards the application of new methods for data visualization and pattern recognition of large datasets as a basis for uncovering interesting biological relationships of potential use in the drug discovery. Candidates should have a Ph.D. in chemistry or related fields with a strong computing background or a Ph.D. in computer science with some familiarity with chemistry. Background in statistics is also desirable but not essential. Interested individuals should send a detailed resume (preferably by email) describing their research experience to: Dr. Gerald M.
