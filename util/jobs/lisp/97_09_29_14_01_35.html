Each of these tutors ~ focuses on the use of mathematical knowledge in reasoning about real world problem situations ~ emphasizes the use of modern computational tools ~ is constructed around a cognitive model of the knowledge students are aquiring The PACT Algebra I Tutor is successfully deployed in 45 middle schools, high schools and colleges in the Pittsburgh region, around the country and in Europe in 1997-1998, with wider dissemination planned in upcoming years. Geometry and Algebra II Tutors are being piloted in Allegheny county with wider dissemination also expected in future years. POSITION A programmer is needed, at either the junior or senior level, to provide general support for the mathematics tutor development project. JUNIOR LEVEL. Primary responsibilities for a programmer hired at the junior level will include: * Porting the tutor interface tools from LISP to JAVA for purposes of cross-platform and web-based deployment. * Development of new tutor interface tools This position requires a BS or equivalent in computer science, cognitive science or cognitive psychology. Experience with the following is desireable: JAVA, LISP/CLOS, C++, any GUI framework, interprocess communications SENIOR LEVEL. Primary responsibilities for a programmer hired at the junior level will include: * Interface development tasks described above * Maintenance of our authoring environment called the Tutor Development Kit * Development of the Tutor Development Kit into a more powerful and user-friendly system The Tutor Development Kit incorporates (1) a production language derived from the ACT-R cognitive theory that is used to model skilled knowledge and interpret student behavior, (2) a mathematical model of learning and performance used to model student learning and (3) curriculum sequencing facilities. In addition to the requirements for the junior position, this position requires experience in AI programming in LISP/CLOS. An MS in computer science, cognitive science or cognitive psychology is desireable, as is experience in production system programming, cognitive modeling, cognitive science research, research programming. The position is available immediately. The initial appointment is for one-year, but additional years are anticipated. A full-time position is available, but a part-time position may be possible.
