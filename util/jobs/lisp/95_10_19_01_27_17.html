The goal of X-33 program is to build a prototype of the next generation reusable launch vehicle that will replace the space shuttle in the next decade. The goal of the Intelligent Maintenance project is to reduce the time needed to maintain and refurbish the vehicle between flights through the use of specialize sensors, vehicle component performance monitoring and automated maintenance scheduling.
