Salary Range: Approximately. $40,000-$55,000 The HPCD Project: The HPCD Project is an ARPA-funded, multi-university, multi-disciplinary research project, whose goal is to study the application of Artificial Intelligence and other areas of Computer Science, of Computational Science (especially Computational Fluid Dynamics), and of high-performance computing to problems of design. Design tasks we are currently working on include design of jet engine inlets and nozzles, container ships, microprocessors, and microlithography processes. The HPCD Project is centered at Rutgers University in New Brunswick, NJ, where the person hired for this position would be located.
