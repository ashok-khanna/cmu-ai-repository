   IBUKI RELEASES PC SCHEME 5.01 FOR IBM COMPATIBLE 486 MACHINES

LOS ALTOS, CA, June 9, 1994--IBUKI announced today that it will release
PC Scheme version 5.01 on July 4, 1995.  The new version of PC Scheme
runs under DOS on all IBM compatable PCs, including those using the
Intel 486 chip, and will now use as much as 4 megabytes of extended
memory.  IBUKI will begin marketing and distributing PC Scheme 5.01
worldwide immediately.

Upgrades to PC Scheme 5.01 are available for existing users of PC Scheme
and IBUKI has instituted a special program for its use by schools teaching
scheme in courses.  Please contact IBUKI for pricing details and to be
placed on our (e)mailing list.
