T: Scheme-like language developed at Yale

lang/scheme/impl/t/
T3.1 is a Scheme-like language developed at Yale. Includes a foreign function (C) interface. The distribution includes a copy of the online version of the T manual and release notes for T3.0 and T3.1. MULT is a multiprocessing version of T for the Encore Multimax.
