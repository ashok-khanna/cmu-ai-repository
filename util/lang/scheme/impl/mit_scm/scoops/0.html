lang/scheme/impl/mit_scm/scoops/
The scoops-7.0.scm in this directory modified by Peter Ross (peter@aipna.ed.ac.uk) to work with MIT Scheme 7.0 and beyond. It is based on a version of scoops written at Texas Instruments and modified by sherin@linc.cis.upenn.edu (who is probably no longer at linc.cis.upenn.edu) to work on an old version of MIT Scheme.
