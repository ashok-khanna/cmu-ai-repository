YASOS: Yet Another Scheme Object System

lang/scheme/oop/yasos/
YASOS (Yet Another Scheme Object System) is fairly functional in style and uses delegation. The implementation includes multiple inheritance and "send to super" and is much smaller than class-based OO systems. Includes sample collection operations over lists, vectors, strings, and simple tables. YASOS is also included as part of SLIB. The article (swob.txt) was first published as [1].
