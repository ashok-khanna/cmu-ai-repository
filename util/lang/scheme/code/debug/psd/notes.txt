A while back Kaelin Colclasure (kaelin@bridge.com) reported troubles
with Psd and named let. It turned out that the problem was not with
named let but with string->list. Below is a patch to correct the problem.
--
pertti
----------------------------------------------------------------------
*** 1.7	1994/02/07 10:08:25
--- primitives.scm	1994/05/04 09:26:41
***************
*** 770,776 ****
  		 (,set-car! (2 ,(assert 1 pair?)))
  		 (,set-cdr! (2 ,(assert 1 pair?)))
  		 (,string (&rest ,(assert 'all char?)))
! 		 (,string->list (1 ,(assert 1 string)))
  		 (,string->number
  		  (1 ,(assert 1 string?))
  		  (2 ,(assert 1 string?)
