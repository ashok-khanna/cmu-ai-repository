This directory contains archives for the comp.lang.lisp.franz newsgroup. The new articles are archived once a week, in a gzipped tar file of the form YY_MM_DD.tgz, where YY, MM, and DD are replaced with the year, month, and day, respectively. Most archiving began with 94_09_14.tgz, which includes articles since 23-JUL-94.3 Note that comp.lang.lisp.franz is for discussion of Franz Lisp, a dialect of Lisp. This newsgroup is not for discussion of Allegro Common Lisp from Franz Inc, but some discussion does tend to creep in here due to name confusion. Allegro CL should be discussed in comp.lang.lisp, or in the allegro-cl@cs.berkeley.edu mailing list.
