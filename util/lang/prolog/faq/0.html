lang/prolog/faq/
This directory contains the Prolog Resource Guide (PRG) maintained by Mark Kantrowitz and the Prolog FAQ maintained by Jamie Andrews. Both FAQs are posted to comp.lang.prolog once a month, and are intended to complement one another. The PRG is also posted to the comp.object.logic newsgroup.
