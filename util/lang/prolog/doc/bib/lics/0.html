LICS: Bibliography for the annual IEEE Symposium on Logic in Computer Science

lang/prolog/doc/bib/lics/
This directory contains a BibTeX bibliography for the annual IEEE Symposium on Logic in Computer Science. About 1/3 of the entries include abstracts. It covers the years 1986 to the present.
