Tech., Haifa Lines: 67 Sender: rick@sparky.sterling.com Approved: rick@sparky.sterling.com Distribution: world Expires: 16 Aug 1994 8:00:00 GMT Message-ID: <2qtjmf$5ep@sparky.sterling.com> NNTP-Posting-Host: sparky.sterling.com Call For Papers 11th Israeli Symposium on Artificial Intelligence Jerusalem, January 17-18, 1995 The conference is the annual meeting of the Israeli Association for Artificial Intelligence which is an affiliate of the Israeli Information Processing Association. Papers are solicited addressing all aspects of AI, including, but not limited to, the following topics: automated reasoning, AI and education, case-based reasoning, cognitive modeling, distributed AI, game-playing, knowledge acquisition, knowledge representation, machine learning, natural language processing, neural networks, planning, probabilistic reasoning, problem solving, robotics, search, temporal reasoning, theorem proving, vision. Submitted papers will be refereed by the program committee. Authors should submit 4 copies of the full paper (in English). Submissions must be printed on 8.5" X 11" or A4 paper using 12 point type. The body of submitted papers must be at most 12 pages, excluding bibliography. Papers should be received by the conference chairman at the following address by August 15th, 1994. Authors will be notified of acceptance by October 15th, 1994. Accepted papers will appear in the conference proceedings.
