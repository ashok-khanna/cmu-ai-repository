This continues a tradition - the seven previous conferences in this series have been notable for attendance and participation from a wide variety of countries. Short Details on IEA/AIE-95: ---------------------------- IEA/AIE-95 will be held in Melbourne Australia during June 1995. Papers are being sought in the general area of application of the techniques from AI to engineering and industrial problems. These papers may be short or long and may be accepted, accepted with changes or accepted as reserve or poster papers.
