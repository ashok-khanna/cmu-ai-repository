The Symposium is sponsored by: The ITESM (Instituto Tecnologico y de Estudios Superiores de Monterrey) in cooperation with the International Joint Conferences on Artificial Intelligence Inc., the American Association for Artificial Intelligence, the Canadian Society for Computational Studies of Intelligence, the European Coordinating Committee for Artificial Intelligence, the International Association of Knowledge Engineers, the Sociedad Mexicana de Inteligencia Artificial, and IBM de Mexico. Papers from all countries are sought that: (1) Present applications of artificial intelligence technology to the solution of problems in industry, business and related areas; (2) Address the issue of transferring the AI Technology; (3) Describe research on innovative AI techniques to accomplish such applications. (Theoretical papers will be considered) Areas of application include but are not limited to: manufacturing, automation, control systems, planning, design, production, distribution, marketing, human resources, finance, stock exchange, international business, environmental control, underwriting, legal aspects, communication media, decission support, digital system design. Technology transfer includes but is not limited to: strategies for introducing and institutionalizing AI technology, human resources development in AI, justification of AI projects, cooperation programs, sociotechnical impact of AI technology. AI techniques include but are not limited to: knowledge-based systems, expert systems, neural networks, fuzzy logic, automated reasoning, computer vision, robotics, natural language, cognitive systems, multimedia, knowledge engineering, computer-human interfaces, tutoring systems, machine learning, special architectures, parallel architectures Persons wishing to submit a paper should send five copies written in English to: Rogelio Soto / Jose M. Sanchez, Program Chairs Centro de Inteligencia Artificial, ITESM, Sucursal de Correos J, Monterrey, N.L. 64849, Mexico. Phone: (52-8) 328-4197 Telefax: (52-8) 328-4189 Net address: r.soto@ieee.org Paper submission: isai@alfa.mty.itesm.mx The paper should specify the area and technique to which it belongs. Papers will be evaluated with respect to their originality, correctness, clarity and relevance. Use a serif type font, size 10, single-spaced with a maximum of 10 pages (single column) or 8 pages (double column). Include your e-mail, fax and phone number. Papers in postscript files will be accepted by electronic mail. Conference papers must be presented in site by the author/co-author. The best papers will be published in a special issue of Integrated Computer- Aided Engineering Journal. Critical dates: Papers must be received by March 19,1994. Authors will be notified of acceptance / rejection by May 20,1994.
