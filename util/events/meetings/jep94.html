This international meeting of the Frenchspeaking community brings together specialists of speech sciences. Topics of particular interest include : speech production, speech perception, phonetics, phonology, studies on lexical,syntactical, and semantical aspects of speech, cognition, oral dialogue and pragmatics, speech analysis, speech coding, noisy speech processing, speech recognition, speech synthesis, speaker and language identification, speech databases, evaluation methods, story and sociology of speech sciences. Papers describing unpublished research in this area are sollicited. Authors must submit four (4) complete copies of their paper (hard copy only), received by January 28, 1994, to : Comite d'Organisation des JEP 94 Secretariat TSS CNET Lannion A Route de Tregastel BP 40 22301 Lannion Cedex France Papers should be written in French, no longer than 6 pages (A4 format), including abstract and keywords, double column, single-spaced, and printed using Times 10 pt. type. Electronic submissions and papers received after the deadlinewill not be accepted.
