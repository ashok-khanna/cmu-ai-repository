Subject matter includes genetic algorithms, evolutionary computation and all related topics and applications. This year, the conference is intended in the first place as a get-together for french speakers, and most presentations will be in french, although papers in english are welcome. In the first half of the conference french researchers will present their ongoing work (no review), while the second half of the conference is reserved for reviewed long papers (8 pages in IEEE double-column format). A CD with software from archive sites will be cut, and tutorials can be organised on september 19th if there is sufficient demand. Fees will be 800 FF including board and accomodation in a student hostel. Papers to be submitted by June 1st, with acceptance notified by Aug 1st. For details please see french Call For Papers you can obtain by ftp to gogol.cenatls.cena.dgac.fr (143.196.1.6), dir /pub/export/EA, files appel_a_communication.ascii and appel_a_communication.ps, or by E-mail to mserver@cenatls.cena.dgac.fr message send-me pub/export/EA/appel_a_communication.ps All enquiries by email to ea@eis.enac.dgac.fr.
