	International Conference on Chinese Computing'94 (ICCC94)

	The Latest Technological Advancement and Applications
			1-4 June 1994, Singapore


			Jointly organised by
  Chinese and Oriental Languages Information Processing Society (Singapore)
	Department of Information Systems and Computer Science, 
	   National University of Singapore(Singapore) 
	 Chinese Information Processing Society (China)

Theme:
The theme of this conference is the application of advanced technologies and 
knowledge to the processing of Chinese, Japanese, Korean and other oriental 
languages. Recent advances and developments in methodologies, systems and 
software and hardware will be emphasized.

Papers: Suggested topics included but are not limited to the following:
*   Computer input/output
*   Font design, coding and  typesetting
*   Properties and statistics of natural language
*   Programming languages
*   Expert systems and knowledge-based systems
*   Database and information system design
*   Speech input and output
*   Natural language processing
*   Machine translation, Evaluation
*   Keyboard and terminal design
*   Character recognition and document reading
*   Computer based education
