With the ever expanding effort to reduce product development cycle times and the critical role of computers used in concurrent engineering, models of manufacturing processes and operations are being integrated into product development systems. This effort has been reflected in efforts in the U.S. to develop centers investigating agile manufacturing methods as well as the on-going international project in Intergrated Manufacturing Systems. The focus of these sessions at the Computers in Engineering Conference centers on how this integration between manufacturing process models and product design methods is being accomplished, environments and systems for virtual manufacturing modeling and simulation, modular manufacturing methods and approaches as well as issues outstanding in virtual manufacturing to be addressed.
