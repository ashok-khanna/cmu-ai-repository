Dear Attendee,

On Thursday 30 September 1993, a Symposium on Advanced Manufacturing Systems 
will be held in Palm Cove in association with  the ETFA'93 conference.

This Symposium will feature presentations by speakers from Australia and Japan 
involved in national projects related to advanced manufacturing 
technologies.

The objective of this Symposium is to provide a platform for practitioners and 
researchers from industry and academia to report and discuss current 
developments in the advanced manufacturing areas.  The emphasis will be on the 
relavance of those technologies to the industry. 

For further information regarding the Symposium program, please contact the 
Symposium chair, the address of whom is given below.

This Symposium is intended to be a closed event with a limited number of 
participants involved.  The Symposium registration fee is A$250.  It includes 
admission to the Symposium, tea breaks, lunch, and a transcript of the 
presentations. ** THE SYMPOSIUM REGISTRATION FEE IS WAIVED FOR THE ETFA'93 
ATTENDEES**.  All ETFA'93 attendees are encouraged to register for this 
Symposium.  Please make sure that we receive your registration form for the 
Symposium within the next two weeks.  For this purpose, please use the 
attached registration form.  It should be emailed or fax to the SAMS'93 
Chairman.

A Symposium dinner will be held on Thursday, 30 Sept.  An additional payment 
of A$65 will be charged for the dinner.

The registration payment can be made at the registration desk. No
personal cheques or payments in currencies other then Australian
Dollars will be accepted. Bank cheques or bank drafts should be 
drawn in Australian Dollars on one of Australian banks. If a cheque
cannot be drawn in the Australian bank, an additional A$40 
processing fee will be charged.  

The advance registration payment should be sent to the Symosium Chair,
Dr Richard Zurawski.


The Kuranda trip will be organised on Thursday 30 September and 
Friday 1 October.  Those who plan to attend the Symposium on Advanced 
Manufacturing Systems will have an option of going to Kuranda on Friday or 
will be refunded.




Siong K. Tang
ETFA'93 Secretary
