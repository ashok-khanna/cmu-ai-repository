Dear fuzzy researchers,

   Existing efforts on fuzzy relational databases has
been conducted in various ways with respect to  a number
 of issues.  Listed below are some more publications
and contributions to  information retrieval, data
representation, data closeness and redundancy, integrity
constraints, and database design:

- Etienne E. Kerre, Guoqing Chen, et al.:

[1! Zenner R., Kerre E.E., De Caluwe R., Practical
determination of document description relations in
document retrieval systems, Proceedings of Workshop on
the Membership Function (EIASM), Brussels, 127-138, 1984.

[2! Zenner R., Kerre E.E., De Caluwe, A new approach to
information retrieval systems using fuzzy expressions,
Fuzzy Sets and Systems, 17, 9-22, 1985.

[3! Kerre E.E., Zenner R., De Caluwe R., The use of fuzzy
set theory in information retrieval and databases: a
survey. JASIS 37, 341-345, 1986.

[4! Kerre E.E., A walk through fuzzy relations and their
applications to information retrieval, medical diagnosis
and expert systems, Proceedings of ISUMA'90, IEEE
Computer Society Press, CA, 84-89, 1990.

[5! Kerre E.E., Introduction to the basic principles of
fuzzy set theory and some of its applications,
Communication & Cognition, Belgium, 1991 (Second edition,
1993).

[6! Chen Guoqing, Vandenbulcke J., Kerre E.E., A step
towards the theory of fuzzy database design, Proceedings
of the 4th World Congress of International Fuzzy Systems
Association (IFSA'91), Brussels, pp44-47, 1991.

[7! Chen Guoqing, Fuzzy data modeling: perspectives,

(Belgium), pp294-343, 1991.

[8! Chen G., Vandenbulcke J., Kerre E.E., A general

model, Journal of the American Society for Information
Science (JASIS), pp304-311, 1992.

[9! Chen G., Kerre E.E., Vandenbulcke J., Fuzzy
functional dependency and its axiomatic system in a fuzzy
relational data model. Proceedings of International
Conference on Information Processing and Management of
Uncertainty (IPMU'92), Spain, pp313-316, 1992.

[10! Chen, G.; Kerre, E. E.; Vandenbulcke, J., The
axiomatic system of fuzzy functional dependency.
Proceedings of International Conference on Information
Systems (ICIS'92),  Dalian Maritime University Publishing
House, 876-880, 1992.

[11! Chen, G.; Kerre, E. E.; Vandenbulcke, J., On the
