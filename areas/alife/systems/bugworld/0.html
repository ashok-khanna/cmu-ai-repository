BUGWORLD: Bug ecology, with predators.

areas/alife/systems/bugworld/
This system simulates a bug ecology, complete with predators. As in Tierra, the bugs are virtual computers. The bugs have to eat food (litter) in a 2d toroidal world, while avoiding predators.
