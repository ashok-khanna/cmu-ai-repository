HUGIN Expert System: Demo versions for Solaris, Sun4, and Windows

areas/expert/systems/hugin/
HUGIN Explorer is a graphical expert system shell, for construction and execution of model based expert systems in domains characterized by inherent uncertainty. This directory contains demo versions of the HUGIN Expert System on the Sparc for SunOS 4.x and Solaris and on the PC under Windows 3.1. Some company literature is also included.
