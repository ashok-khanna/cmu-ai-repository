CAM: Cellular Automata Simulator

areas/ca/systems/cam/
The CAM Simulator program is a general purpose cellular automata simulation environment based on CAM-6 (see Toffoli, T. and Margolus, N., Cellular Automata Machines, MIT Press (1987), for details). It is not downwardly compatible with the original. The simulator is an interactive FORTH interpreter.
