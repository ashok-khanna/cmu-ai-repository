SNLP: Domain-Independent Systematic Nonlinear Planner.

areas/planning/systems/snlp/
This directory contains SNLP, a domain-independent systematic nonlinear planner that is both complete and sound. No longer maintained. UCPOP is upward compatible to SNLP (and better in all respects too).
