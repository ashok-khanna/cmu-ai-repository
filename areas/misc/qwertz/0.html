QWERTZ Toolbox: AI Routines for Standard ML

areas/misc/qwertz/
The QWERTZ toolbox is a library of Standard ML modules with an emphasis on symbolic Artificial Intelligence programming, (including implementations of heuristic search and an ATMS reason maintenance system).
