TRUCKWORLD: Testbed for AI Planning Programs

areas/testbeds/trckwrld/
TRUCKWORLD is a simulated world intended to provide a testbed for AI planning programs, where the planning agent is a truck with arms that roams around the simulated world. It includes an X interface.
