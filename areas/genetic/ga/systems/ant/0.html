ANT: PC implementation of 'John Muir Trail' experiment

areas/genetic/ga/systems/ant/
ANT is a PC version of the 'John Muir Trail' experiment. It simulates trail-following behavior and evolution by natural selection. A companion paper describes the work. Turbo C source and an MSDOS executable are included. The UNIX port is incomplete.
