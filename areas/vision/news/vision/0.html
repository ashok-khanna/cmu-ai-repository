areas/vision/news/vision/
This directory contains archives for the comp.ai.vision newsgroup. The new articles are archived once a week, in a gzipped tar file of the form YY_MM_DD.tgz, where YY, MM, and DD are replaced with the year, month, and day, respectively. Local archiving began with 94_09_14.tgz, which includes articles since 23-JUL-94. Archives from March 1988 through the end of August 1994 (in the files YYdigest.tgz, where YY is the year) were obtained from the Vision List archive, which provides comp.ai.vision in digest format.
