EDICT: Japanese/English Dictionary

areas/nlp/corpora/dicts/edictj/
EDICT is the outcome of a voluntary project to produce a public domain Japanese/English Dictionary in machine-readable form. It was intended initially for use with MOKE (Mark's Own Kanji Editor) and related software such as JDIC and JREADER, however it has come to be used in a large number of packages. The dictionary has 77,406 entries.
