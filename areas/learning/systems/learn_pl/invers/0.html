INVERS: Two versions of the two main operators for inverse resolution.

areas/learning/systems/learn_pl/invers/
This directory contains INVERS, an implementation of Steven Muggleton's absorption and intra-construction operators for inverse resolution based on the representation change idea of Celine Rouveirol and Jean-Francois Puget.
