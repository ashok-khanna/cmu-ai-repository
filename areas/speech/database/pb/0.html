Peterson Barney: Vowel formant frequency database

areas/speech/database/pb/
This directory contains the Peterson and Barney vowel formant frequency database, as used in their 1952 JASA paper. Peterson and Barney measured the frequency and amplitude of F1, F2 and F3 for 10 vowels and 76 speakers.
