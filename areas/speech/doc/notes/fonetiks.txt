In response to a recent query on this list regarding the 'foNETiks' electronic 
newlsetter, here are some details. 

'foNETiks' is a monthly newsletter containing information about meetings, job 
opportunities, and other issues of interest to those working and studying in 
the areas of phonetics, phonology and speech science. The newsletter appears   
in the first  week of each month, and the deadline for submissions is normally 
around the 25th of the preceding month. If you wish to join the subscription 
list, send the following 1 line message to 'mailbase@uk.ac.mailbase'          

join fonetiks your_first_name your_second_name 

For example;   join fonetiks fred bloggs
 
if you wish to submit an item for inclusion in foNETiks, please send it to 
'fonetiks@uk.ac.mailbase'.  The list is moderated, and, if appropriate, the 
item will be retained and included in the next issue.

Please contact us (on 'fonetiks-request@uk.ac.mailbase') if you have any 
difficulty subscribing to the list, or if you need any further information.
