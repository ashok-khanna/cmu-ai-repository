RECNET: Connectionist speech recognition system using recurrent nets.

areas/speech/systems/recnet/
This directory contains RECNET, a connectionist speech recognition system. It performs phone and word recognition for the TIMIT and Resource Management databases using recurrent nets. Known to be of limited use.
