LPC-10: Implementation of DoD LPC-10 2400 bps Voice Coder

areas/speech/systems/lpc/
This directory contains an implementation of the U.S. Department of Defense LPC-10 2400 bps Voice Coder (version 55). Also included are some sample speech files, before and after coding, a file of Frequently Asked Questions items for LPC and CELP, and ABTOOL 1.2 (beta), a Sun GUI tool for playing listening comparisons between speech files.
